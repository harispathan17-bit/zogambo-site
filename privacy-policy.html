1. Who We Are

Zogambo Studios LLP ("Zogambo," "we," "us," or "our") is an independent mobile gaming studio incorporated in India and engaged in the development and publishing of casual mobile games for iOS and Android platforms. Our registered office is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

This Privacy Policy applies to all products, services, mobile applications, and websites operated by us, including our website at zogambo.in and all mobile games we publish (collectively, the "Services").

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy is an electronic record under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), along with all applicable rules made thereunder. It is generated by a computer system and does not require any physical or digital signatures.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using any of our Services, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services immediately.

This policy covers how we collect, store, use, share, and protect your personal data — both through our online Services (website and mobile apps) and through any offline interactions such as email correspondence.

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Name and email address when you contact us or correspond with us
  • Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to developer@zogambo.in
  • Any information you voluntarily share through our website or in-game contact features

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our games or visit our website, we and our third-party service partners may automatically collect:

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, Google Advertising ID on Android) and Device Identifier
  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country and region level)
  • App version, installation source, and session metadata
  • Gameplay data — levels reached, session duration, feature usage, and in-game events
  • Crash logs and diagnostic performance reports
  • In-app purchase records (processed by Apple App Store or Google Play; we do not store payment card details)

Information from Third-Party Partners

  • Attribution data from ad networks, indicating how you discovered our games
  • Aggregated analytics data from analytics service providers
  • Social login data where you use a third-party sign-in, subject to your privacy settings on that platform

4. Why We Collect Your Information

We collect and process your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our games and website
  • Personalise your gameplay experience and serve the correct app version for your device
  • Analyse game performance, identify bugs, and continuously improve our products
  • Respond to your support requests and communications promptly
  • Serve in-game advertisements through our ad network partners
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing and publisher campaigns
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, cheating, and security incidents
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations under Indian law, including the DPDP Act, 2023 and the IT Act, 2000

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Game operation: Device and gameplay data is used to serve the correct app version, maintain game progress, and ensure a stable experience.
  • Analytics & improvement: Aggregated, anonymised usage data helps us understand feature engagement and guides our product roadmap.
  • Advertising: Advertising IDs are used to serve and measure in-game ads. We do not link Advertising IDs to your personally identifiable information.
  • Communication: If you contact us, we use your email address solely to respond. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.
  • Security: IP addresses and device data are used to detect anomalous activity, prevent fraud, and protect our systems and players.
  • Legal compliance: We may process and retain data as required by applicable Indian law, including the IT Act, 2000 and the DPDP Act, 2023.

7. Third-Party Advertising & Analytics

Our games contain advertisements served by third-party ad networks, primarily Google AdMob. These networks may collect your Advertising ID, IP address, and device data to deliver targeted or contextual advertisements. Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

You can limit interest-based advertising at any time:

  • iOS (14+): Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

Opting out does not remove ads from our games; it means the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests. We use Google Analytics on our website to understand visitor behaviour. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website at zogambo.in uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic website functionality — these cannot be disabled without affecting site performance
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site, powered by Google Analytics

You can control or disable cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features but will not affect your ability to download or play our mobile games.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for a general audience and are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are between 13 and 18 years of age, we recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy with a parent or guardian before using our Services.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that data. If you believe we may have such information, please contact us immediately at developer@zogambo.in.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, provide our Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Gameplay and device data may be retained in anonymised or aggregated form for analytical purposes after the original personal data has been deleted or de-identified.

To request deletion of your personal data, email us at developer@zogambo.in with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe consistent with our legal obligations.

11. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian law and, where relevant, the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to any applicable legal retention obligations
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time where processing is consent-based, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of processing, including for direct marketing purposes
  • Right to data portability: Where applicable, receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please write to us at developer@zogambo.in. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request and will respond within 30 days.

12. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include data encryption in transit and at rest, restricted access controls, firewalls, and regular security reviews.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take all reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. You transmit information to us at your own risk. We recommend not sharing your account credentials with anyone.

If you suspect any unauthorised access to your account or a security incident involving our Services, please notify us immediately at developer@zogambo.in.
