Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Zogambo Studios

At Zogambo Studios, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or play our games.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information  — If you email us directly, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message.
  • Usage data  — Our games may collect anonymised gameplay data such as session length, level progress, and device type to help us improve the player experience.
  • Advertising data  — Our games may display ads served by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These networks may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and communications sent to us
  • Improve and optimise our games and website
  • Understand how players interact with our games
  • Serve relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Our games and website may integrate third-party services including ad networks, analytics providers, and publishing partners. These third parties operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

Third-party services we may use include Google AdMob, Unity Analytics, and similar platforms commonly used in mobile game development.

4. Children's Privacy

Some of our games may be suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Anonymised gameplay data may be retained for longer periods for analytics purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at developer@zogambo.in.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out at developer@zogambo.in.
