1. Who We Are

Zogambo Studios LLP ("Zogambo," "we," "us," or "our") is an independent mobile gaming studio incorporated in India, engaged in the development and publishing of casual mobile games for iOS and Android platforms. Our registered office is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.

This Privacy Policy applies to all products, services, mobile applications, and websites operated by us, including our website at zogambo.in and all mobile games we publish (collectively, the "Services").

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy is an electronic record under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), along with all applicable rules made thereunder.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using any of our Services, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

This policy covers how we collect, store, use, share, and protect your personal data — through our online Services (website and mobile apps) and any offline interactions such as email correspondence.

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name and email address when you contact or correspond with us
  • Messages, feedback, and support requests sent to developer@zogambo.in
  • Any information you voluntarily share through our website or in-game features

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) and Device Identifier
  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country and region level)
  • App version, installation source, and session metadata
  • Gameplay data — levels, session duration, feature usage, in-game events
  • Crash logs and diagnostic performance reports
  • In-app purchase records processed by Apple App Store or Google Play — we do not store payment card details

Information from Third-Party Partners

  • Attribution data from ad networks indicating how you discovered our games
  • Aggregated analytics data from analytics service providers
  • Social login data where you use a third-party sign-in, subject to your privacy settings on that platform

4. Why We Collect Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our games and website
  • Personalise your gameplay experience and serve the correct app version
  • Analyse performance, identify bugs, and improve our products
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Serve in-game advertisements through our ad network partners
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing and publisher campaigns
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud and security incidents
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations under Indian law

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Game operation: Device and gameplay data is used to serve the correct app version and ensure a stable experience.
  • Analytics: Anonymised usage data helps us understand feature engagement and guides our product roadmap.
  • Advertising: Advertising IDs are used to serve and measure in-game ads. We do not link them to your personally identifiable information.
  • Communication: If you contact us, we use your email solely to respond. We do not send unsolicited marketing emails.
  • Security: IP addresses and device data help detect anomalous activity and prevent fraud.
  • Legal compliance: We may retain data as required by the IT Act, 2000 and the DPDP Act, 2023.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their independent marketing purposes. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We engage trusted third-party providers who process data on our behalf under written data processing agreements. They may only use data to perform services for us.

Third-Party Services We Currently Use

  • Google AdMob — serving in-game advertisements
  • Google Firebase — app analytics and crash reporting
  • Unity Analytics — gameplay event tracking and session data
  • AppsFlyer or Adjust — marketing attribution (subject to change)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose data to government or law enforcement authorities if required by applicable law, a valid governmental request, or a lawful court order under the IT Act, 2000, the DPDP Act, 2023, or any other applicable Indian statute.

Business Transfers

If Zogambo Studios LLP undergoes a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may transfer to the successor entity. We will notify you where reasonably practicable.

7. Third-Party Advertising & Analytics

Our games contain advertisements served by third-party ad networks including Google AdMob. These networks may collect your Advertising ID, IP address, and device data to serve targeted or contextual ads. Their practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

To limit interest-based advertising:

  • iOS (14+): Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

Opting out does not remove ads from our games — it means the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website at zogambo.in uses cookies to improve your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality and cannot be disabled without affecting site performance
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behaviour, powered by Google Analytics

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. This may affect website features but will not affect your ability to use our mobile games.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data has been inadvertently collected, we will delete it immediately.

Users aged 13–18 should review this policy with a parent or guardian before using our Services.

If you believe we may have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us at developer@zogambo.in.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Gameplay and device data may be retained in anonymised or aggregated form for analytical purposes after original personal data has been deleted.

To request deletion of your data, email us at developer@zogambo.in with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

11. Your Rights

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing

To exercise any of these rights, write to developer@zogambo.in. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request and will respond within 30 days.

12. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organisational security measures including data encryption, restricted access controls, firewalls, and regular security reviews.

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data. You transmit information to us at your own risk. We recommend not sharing account credentials with anyone.

If you suspect any unauthorised access or a security incident involving our Services, please notify us immediately at developer@zogambo.in.
